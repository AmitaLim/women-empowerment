"Women's march in london and across the US"

Studies and surveys have shown that the biggest challenges for women in this 21th century are:

  • Career opportunities
  • Gender Bias
  • Gender pay gap
  • Harassment
  • domestic violence
  • Female genital mutilation
  • Trafficking
  • Low rate conviction rape
  • Poor parliamentary representation

What can every woman do to change that, and get what she deserves?

  1. Know yourself and Believe in yourself
  2. Be courageous and have a voice
  3. Get organised, Stand up for yourself and lead by example
  4. Invest your time and money to empower other women
  5. Challenge gender norms (and the status quo)
  6. Do it over and over and over again
